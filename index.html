Instructions

Watering

Best practice is weekly checks of soil dampness, if dry then water over sink and let excess drain. Repeat WEEKLY.

Pest Management

Plants can be sprayed heavily with water to knock off any bugs/mites. Be sure to get under leaves to destroy any nests or webs.

Lighting

Place your plant in a east or west facing direction to maximize your sun intake. Doing this also feeds and generates the energy needed for your plant to thrive.
